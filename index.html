Meet the Couple

Scott met Kelly in 8th grade while attending middle school at Penn's Grove Middle School in Oxford, Pennsylvania. Their friendship started when they both were members of the Oxford Area High School Marching Hornets Drum Line, the Oxford Hornets Swim Team, and worked both Stage Crew/Talent in the musicals and plays at OAHS. Their friendship continued to grow through the high school years, especially while attending heavy metal festivals such as OZZFEST and Mayhem Fest. After high school Scott attended Towson University and graduated with a bachelors degree in Electronic Media and Film. He now is employed at R-V Industries as a Pre-Fabrication Forman. With their mutual love of Philadelphia sports, heavy metal music, each other's families, and their cats, they know it was meant to be.

Kelly was born and lived with her family through 7th grade in Delaware County. After moving to Oxford, Kelly and Scott initially met in 8th grade in Mr. Eason’s health class at Penn’s grove Middle School. Kelly and Scott became fast friends while participating in the OAHS marching band drum line, the OAHS swim team and stage crew. After high school Kelly attended West Chester University and graduated with a BS, majoring in Dietetics and a minor in chemistry. She is now an Insurance Claims Specialist. Kelly played a variety of sports including softball, swimming and volleyball from the time she was old enough to play t-ball through her senior year of high school and played on club teams through college. Kelly is a lifelong Philadelphia Eagles, Flyers and of course- PHILLIES fan!!! It is her dream to be married to her best friend on their favorite baseball team’s home field!
